Welcome to the AAC-Clydespace Parts and Document Register


This registry contains registered parts and document numbers. You can check out new part numbers and document numbers here.

How to use:

To create a new part number or document number, go to the 'add' pages in the navigation bar above. Once a part is created, you will be taken to the part number page, where you can add electromechanical info, vendor details and online resources, including links to sharepoint documents or bitbucket repositories. Regular users cannot delete or modify part numbers.
